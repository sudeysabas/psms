This game is called “Dragon Realm”. The player is on an Island of dragons. The dragons live in different caves with their treasure. The friendly dragon will share their treasure with you. The other dragon is hungry and will gobble up the player when they enter the cave. A flow chart is useful tool that allows you to see a clear picture of all the possible actions, options or steps in your game and where they will lead.
